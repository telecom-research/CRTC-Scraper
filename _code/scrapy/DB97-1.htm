DECISION
Ottawa, 14 January 1997
DecisionCRTC 97-1
Native Communication Inc.
Thompson, Manitoba
Revocation
Native Communication Inc. has requested the revocation of its English- and Native-language radio network licence for the purpose of broadcasting radio programming of specific interest to the aboriginal population of northern Manitoba.

The Commission notes that this programming entitled "OWE-TA-MA-KE-WOK" wasdiscontinued in November 1995.

In view of the licensee's request and pursuant to paragraph 9(1)(e) and subsection 24(1) of the Broadcasting Act, the Commission hereby revokes the licence issued to Native Communication Inc. for the above-mentioned network.

Allan J. Darling
Secretary General
